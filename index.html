Monte Carlo Darts

by Cristian Barrera, for Multimedia Programming 100 extra credit

This Web page generates random points within a square and reports the number of points that land inside the square. Simply enter the desired number of points and click the button labeled "Click to generate the points". You may click the button multiple times to continue adding points and accumulating counts. If you wish to start with a new square, click on the button labeled "Clear points and reset counts."
